SAP Practice

Client Focus: Our clients are large multinational companies, which have selected SAP as their enterprise foundation. These companies have a common and particular set of needs and opportunities. When we first engage, our clients are typically undergoing a period of significant change driven by M&A activity, market upheaval or new legislation. Most often, change has already started to happen at a structural level and our first task is to rapidly transform back office systems and processes to catch up and form a foundation for the future; subsequent transformation programs are then used to deliver additional benefits and ultimately to provide competitive advantage.

SAP Leverage: Large companies, which have selected SAP, have done so because they recognize the value and differentiation that the best ERP application in the market can provide. In fact, a generic approach to ERP dilutes the value that an SAP customer has paid for. PETERSON TECHNOLOGY PARTNERS is totally focused on delivering the value that SAP is uniquely able to provide, whilst taking account of its limitations and of the potential of complementary applications. Our approach is to leverage and work with the delivered functionality, not to work around or redevelop it. This requires a deep and up to date understanding of exactly what SAP can and can't deliver. This approach ensures that as both SAP's product offering and a client's particular scope are extended and developed, maximum benefit can be derived at minimum cost and timescale.

Services Integration: Whilst our chosen tool is SAP, our objective is to deliver business benefit. Fundamental to our approach is the provision of a project team, methodology and toolkit to support the delivery of a business transformation as opposed to simply a configured system. Increasingly, the configured system is largely delivered through our industry blueprint program and our real task is to introduce our clients to best practice processes rather than to design new ones. Whilst a deep understanding of SAP is absolutely vital for this integrated approach, so is a clear recognition that the technology is a means to an end. That end is to inform and empower business leaders to enable them to formulate and execute their strategies as cost effectively and quickly as possible.

Adherence to these principles has enabled us to create a company that leads our industry in terms of Innovation, Business Benefit and Delivery.
